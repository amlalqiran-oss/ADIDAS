03 / Returns

RETURNS & EXCHANGES

30-DAY RETURN POLICY

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, you can return your item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with all tags attached.

How to Return

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Order History
  3. Select items to return
  4. Print prepaid shipping label
  5. Drop off at any carrier location

Refund Timeline

  • Processing: 3-5 business days
  • Refund: 5-10 business days to original payment
  • Confirmation: Email notification sent

MEASURING TIPS

  • - Measure in the afternoon when feet are at their largest
  • - Wear the socks you plan to use with the shoes
  • - Stand on a flat surface with your weight evenly distributed
  • - If between sizes, size up for athletic footwear
